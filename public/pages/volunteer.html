Volunteer with Druidis

Want to help the planet? Volunteer with Druidis!

Druidis has many opportunities for volunteering. These include: curating content, writing articles, moderating communities, outreach, marketing, promotion on social media, blogging, vlogging, and much more.

If you're a content creator you should consider sponsoring us, collaborating with us, or featuring us to your audience. Spreading awareness of our project can help us a lot.

Community Moderators & Handlers

Are you just looking to get help Druidis' mission any way you can, or enjoy managing communities? We're looking to promote mature, healthy environments, and providing our mods with the right tools to achieve this.

We take community moderating a bit more seriously than other social media sites, but it's something that anyone can do to support our cause.

Activists

PLEASE contact us if you volunteer as an activist, such as for environmental or social causes. If nothing else, we're just happy that you're involved in activism and will show our gratitude. But generally, we're trying to foster relationships with activists and find ways to collaborate as our project grows.

Project Management and Social Outreach

Finding and reaching out to the right people is one of our first priorities. We want to work with good talent and that can only happen if have someone to contact and work with them.

This role involves working directly with key people in a variety organizations such as influencers, content creators, journalists, etc. You may be responsible for contacting people, discussing opportunities, coordinating projects, and maintaining a dialog when appropriate.

Tell People About Us

Sometimes, the best thing you can do for a project is to bring attention to it. We need people to talk about us, even if that's just telling your friends and family, posting about it on social media, or involving a community you're already a part of.

If you already have a large established audience (influencers, journalists, writers, etc), please let us know and we'll try to find ways to work with your community.
