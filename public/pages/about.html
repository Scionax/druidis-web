About Druidis

You eliminate 1 pound of C02 every minute of using Druidis.
We plant trees and invest in renewables that offset the CO2 burden.

Our mission is to make helping the planet as easy as humanly possible, so we made it possible to improve the environment just by spending time on social media.

How? Simple. For every minute you spend on Druidis, Druidis commits to removing 1 pound of CO2 from the atmosphere. Enough to offset the carbon footprint of three people with 1 hour per day. You're being environmentally responsible by browsing the web!

Using this 1-minute metric, Druidis has a benchmark for how many trees we need to plant or which types of renewable resources we need to invest in.
You're also planting trees by being here, which has additional benefits!
We're making social media environmentally beneficial.

Removing CO2 isn't the only benefit that you get from using our services. Your activity translates into trees getting planted.

Planting trees has several amazing benefits. It creates habitats for animals, expands and improves ecosystems, saves water, prevents pollution, prevents erosion, conserves energy, improves mental health, cleans the air, provides oxygen, and much more.

There are areas of the world that benefit even more from trees being planted, and that's where Druidis is focused on tree planting through the organizations we plant trees with.

Even though environmentalists often focus on greenhouse gases, it's important to remember that trees do a lot of good for other reasons!
Druidis was inspired by Ecosia. A search engine that plants trees.
You should check them out. They're an excellent service.

Druidis was inspired by Ecosia's ingenious idea. Before us, they were converting the profits from their search engine into planting trees and investing in renewables. We ran with the idea, but for social media.

If you like what we've doing here, make sure to take a look at Ecosia. For best results, set them to your default search engine in your browser.

Druidis is taking REAL action to prevent the spread of disinformation.
We also intend to work with experts that can ensure we're doing it correctly.

Disinformation is a serious problem. Rich special interests have been spreading disinformation for decades, and social media has become a primary target.

Unlike certain other social media platforms, Druidis won't ban experts that are investigating the impacts of disinformation. We *want* to work with people who are addressing the issue.

Druidis is highly curated from the top down, and anonymous accounts can't influence the primary content. This makes it MUCH harder for bad actors to spread lies.

We also intend to maintain features that will protect our users and equip us with the tools to track and penalize bad actors.
