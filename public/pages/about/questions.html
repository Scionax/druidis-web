Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can't regular users post topics on the main forums?
A: To protect against disinformation, we restrict public posting in critical sections.

Druidis cares about the issue of disinformation. We are highly discouraged by other social media platforms that have chosen to insufficiently address this problem.

The issue can be solved in many ways, but to start, Druidis holds user accounts to very high accountability to post in critical areas. This prevents nearly all of the typical abuse seen on other sites.

We realize that some of these measures prevent actions that could otherwise be useful, but we're deciding to balance this tradeoff as best we can moving forward.
